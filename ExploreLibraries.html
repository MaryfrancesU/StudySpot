University of Rochester Libraries

Explore the variety of libraries and study places our institution has to offer!

The University of Rochester is a research institution located in the city of Rochester in New York. Many of our students are highly motivated individuals that wish to become professionals in their respective fields and make a change in the world.
Our mission is to provide the environment for them to study and become those inspiring individuals for the society.
The UR study spot offers the following libraries for the students to book their places and work depending on their spot preference and way of studying.
Note: You may read over our selection of the libraries, but our services are only offered to the currently enrolled UR students.
For more specific information on the libraries please see: University of Rochester Libraries
Meliora!
If Entrepreneurship and Inspiration are your mantra, and you enjoy studying in a non-quiet area, join us at iZone! From artsy walls to warm people who are cooking up their next big idea, you will always find a good atmosphere to sit and study in.
PRR is one of the quieter study spaces in Rush Rhees and its aesthetics attract students every day. The dim lights and the atmosphere make the place resemble Hogwarts in the small town of Rochester.
Join us in the home of the multiple collections of DVDs, books, journals, musical scores and artists' books. If you enjoy studying in an artsy and yet calm place, the Art and Music Library is the right place for you.
If you like the hustle and bustle but you also enjoy a quiet place in the middle of the university "traffic", then Q&i is the best place for you! You can get your work done AND meet up with your friends since it is one of the connecting points with the tunnels.
What better place to sit and pull an all-nighter than the Rush Rhees library that is open 24 hours? Gleason offers a wide range of study spots from quiet areas to conversational tones and it is certain you will always find someone to talk to and work with!
