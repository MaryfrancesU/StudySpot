Welcome to StudySpot!

Studying has never been smoother.

Have you ever wandered around your campus libraries desperately hoping you can find an available place to sit and study? Have you ever wished you would
not have to spend this much of your time trying to find a place you can be productive in?
If your answer is yes to both questions, then we have a solution for you! UR Study Spot is the University of Rochester's main portal
for signing up and booking your favorite places to study in for a couple of times! The system updates itself in real-time and now you do not even have
to spend precious time looking for a table to study! Choose from our wide array of options depending on the library, noise level and time!
We are here to support your studying at the best of our abilities! Your time is efficient and we are here to make sure you make the most of your UR experience!
The University of Rochester offers a wide selection of libraries in which to study depending on the students' preferences and if you are not about to scroll through ALL of the libraries offered at the UR, do not fret!
We will find your favorite study spot through your noise-level preference,
your favorite library and the time of the day you are free. Even if the system shows that are not too many free tables, our systems assigns a cap of a couple of hours
allowed for a student to book a study spot, so you will always find a place to sit and finish up your work!
How to use our services:
  1. Sign up using your university email! (Only currently enrolled UR students can use our website)
  2. Log in using your set username and password
  3. Check out our Bookings Page and in the case you find your "soulmate" spot, make a booking
  4. You can always go back and book other appointments or check your upcoming bookings
  5. Enjoy your time with us and thank you for using our services :)
