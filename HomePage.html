Have you ever wandered around your campus libraries desperately hoping you can find an available place to sit and study?
Have you ever wished you wouldn't have to spend so much of your time trying to find a place you can be productive in?

UR StudySpot is the University of Rochester's main portal for, well, booking your favorite study spot! With a system that updates itself real-time, and a wide array of spots to choose from, you no longer have to spend your precious time, wandering around campus, looking for a place to study! We are here to support your studying to the best of our abilities, and to make sure you make the most of your UR experience!
The University of Rochester offers a wide selection of libraries and buildings, perfectly matched to your preferences,. Do you have a preferred location? Need computers? Want to be able to snack while you study? Just let us know and we'll do the filtering for you!
Check out our Bookings Page, find your "soulmate" spot, and book it! You can always make multiple bookings or view your profile to kee track of your upcoming appointments. Enjoy your time with us and thank you for using our services :)
